📋 How to Add Shifts

Shifts are managed through Vera (Boris AI). Send a message to add shifts for today or any date.

"Add intake shifts for today:
- Richard Pena: 9am-6pm
- Luis Vivas: 8am-5pm
- Orlando Plasencia: 10am-7pm"

Or ask: "Show me today's intake shifts" or "Clear shifts for April 20"

⏰ Current Behavior

With shifts configured: Dashboard uses exact scheduled hours.
Without shifts: Dashboard estimates hours from activity timestamps.
